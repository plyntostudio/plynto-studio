Our Process

How we scope
and deliver.

Every engagement — from a ₹5,000 landing page to a ₹50,000+ custom platform — runs through the same four stages.

01

Discovery & scoping

A structured conversation about your goals, users, and constraints. We ask the questions that matter before anything is priced.

02

Fixed-price proposal in 48h

A written scope document with deliverables, timeline, and a firm price — sent within two business days.

03

Sprints with weekly previews

Real, working software delivered every week. You track progress against the agreed scope, not against vague status updates.

04

Launch + 4 months support

Documented, deployed, and tested handoff — followed by four months of included support for fixes and adjustments.
