In-service flow

The app should support the staff, not replace the restaurant.

01

Scan and land on the right branch menu instantly

Guests do not need to download anything. Table number, service time, and local availability are already attached.

02

Filter fast for dietary constraints and browsing mood

Guests can move from categories to "show me something light and vegetarian" without asking staff to decode the menu.

03

Call the server only when human help adds value

Service requests, refills, birthday plating, and bill prompts can be requested without breaking the meal rhythm.
