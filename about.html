Our story

Built from the frustration of
bad agency experiences.

We started Plynto after watching businesses get burned by agencies that overpromised, underdelivered, and disappeared after launch. The problems were always the same: unclear scope, hourly billing surprises, slow communication, and no accountability post-handoff.

So we built the agency we'd want to hire. Fixed pricing. Direct access. Structured delivery. And four months of post-launch support on every single project — because software isn't done when it ships.

We're based across India and work fully remote. Every project is founder-led, which means you talk to the person actually building your product — not a sales rep or a project manager relaying messages.

How we operate

Principles we don't
compromise on.

01

Fix the price before starting

Every project gets a written proposal with a firm price before any work begins. We won't start a project we haven't properly scoped, and we won't bill you for time we didn't forecast.

02

Ship working software, not slide decks

Weekly previews are real, functional builds — not Figma screens or progress presentations. You should be able to click through your product every single week.

03

Stay accountable after launch

Post-launch support isn't a paid tier — it's a standard 4-month commitment on every project. Production bugs get fixed. Tweaks get made. We don't disappear.

04

Communicate directly

No account managers. No ticket systems. You communicate directly with the people building your product. Decisions happen faster. Misunderstandings get caught sooner.

05

Build for handoff, not dependency

Every project we deliver is documented, clean, and deployable by your team or another developer. We don't build systems that only we can maintain — that's not in your interest.
